Welcome to

Monté International School

Monté International is a co-educational & semi-residential school, established under the aegis of K.N. Educational Society, as the latest associate of the Montessori Group of Schools. The school is spread over a verdant campus in lush green environs, on the outskirts of Kurnool city — embodying state-of-the-art facilities in a specifically designed ambience replete with centralized AC classrooms and unique sporting facilities.

Through modern learning-teaching techniques, Monté endeavours to make education a happy, value-based and progressive journey that creates the global citizens of tomorrow.

Monté About

Explore, Experience & Excel at Monté
