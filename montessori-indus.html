About the campus

A home away from
home, since 1991.

Set on a 30-acre green estate, Montessori Indus is a fully residential school affiliated to CBSE. It embraces the Montessori teaching method — a student-centric approach that fosters independence, critical thinking and a love for exploration.

With separate secure hostels for boys and girls, dedicated science labs, a well-stocked library, a swimming pool and expansive playgrounds, day-to-day life balances rigorous academics with sport, music and the arts.

MINDS · Elite Competitive Track

Engineered for
the top.

Montessori Indus Network for Dynamic Students — the upgraded, top-tier 11th & 12th track within Indus, equipped with the latest amenities and built to bridge school with national competitive exams.

Operating on a philosophy of "differentiated schooling," MINDS groups students by learning capacity and problem-solving speed rather than a rigid uniform standard — prioritising deep conceptual logic, analytical thinking and multi-disciplinary application over memorisation.

Run in collaboration with national bodies like Aakash Medical and IIT-JEE Foundations, the track is recognised across Rayalaseema for city-topping scores and placements into IITs, NITs and top medical colleges.
